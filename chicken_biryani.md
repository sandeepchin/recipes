
# Chicken Biryani Recipe

## Custom recipe by Sandeep Chintabathina

This is chicken biryani recipe modeled after the Hyderabadi biryani. I have customized it slightly to match my taste and availability of ingredients.

List of ingredients (Family style 5+ servings)

- 4 lbs of dark meat chicken (Boneless if you prefer)
- 3 cups of Basmati rice
- 1 red onion or 1 cup of fried onion strings
- 1 serrano pepper or 2 thai chili peppers
- 3 cilantro sprigs
- 2 mint sprigs
- 1 large tomato or 2 small tomatoes
- 2 tablespoon Hawaiian sea salt or medium grain rock salt
- 2 tablespoon cayenne pepper powder 
- 1 tablespoon turmeric powder
- 1 tablespoon cumin powder
- 1 tablespoon allspice powder (could be substituted with cinnamon and nutmeg powder)
- 1 tablespoon black pepper powder
- 2 tablespoons ginger garlic paste
- 2 limes
- 1 cup plain yogurt
- 6 cloves of garlic smashed
- 3 tablespoons ghee or cooking oil

For tempering

- 6 cloves
- 8 cardamom pods
- 6 cinnamon sticks
- 4 anise pods
- 1 tablespoon cumin seeds
- 10 bay leaves
- 1 tablespoon ghee or cooking oil

## Procedure

### Marination

1. Cut the chicken up. If using thighs, cut them into 2 or 3 pieces. Leg pieces can be left alone.
2. Rinse the chicken until the water is clear and drain the water.
3. Choose a dish that you will using to marinate the chicken in the refrigerator for at least 8 hours. 
4. Place the chicken in the dish and add turmeric and mix it well. Turmeric has antiseptic properties. Some people may not like turmeric flavor. If this is the case, you can rinse off the turmeric after a few minutes and drain the water.
5. Add all the dry ingredients like salt, cayenne pepper, cumin powder, allspice powder, black pepper powder to the chicken.
6. Add all the wet ingredients like ginger garlic paste, yogurt, and mashed cloves of garlic to the chicken.
7. Squeeze the two limes and add the juice to the chicken marinade.
8. Cut up the serrano pepper or thai chili peppers and add to the marinade.
9. Cut up cilantro including the stems and add it to the marinade.
10. Pluck mint leaves and add it to the marinade.
11. Mix the marinade well and leave it overnight or for 8 hours in the refrigerator (not in the freezer).

### Cooking

1. In a large pot, heat 1-2 tablespoons of ghee or cooking oil.
2. Add the chicken soaked in the marinade to the pot and mix well.
3. Close the pot and cook on medium setting for 20 minutes.
4. As the chicken cooks, you can cook the basmati rice in the rice cooker or however you cook rice. Add 4 cups of water to 3 cups of rice along with some salt and let the rice cooker finish the cooking.
4. Continuing with our chicken, dice the tomatoes and add them to the pot and cook for another 10 minutes. The tomatoes add more flavor to the broth.
5. Taste the chicken to check whether the pieces are tender and cooked. If they are cooked, turn off the stove.
5. In a skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of ghee and roast anise first followed by cinnamon sticks, cardamom, cloves, cumin, and bay leaves. The order would be helpful as some spices burn easily than the others.
6. Add the roasted spices to the cooked chicken and stir. The tempering gives additional flavor to the chicken.
7. Cut the onion into thin long strips. Alternatively, if you have fried onion strings you can just add them to the cooked chicken.
8. In the same skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of ghee or oil and fry the onions until they are carmelized. Add them to the cooked chicken. 

### Final step

1. You will now mix the rice and the chicken together to make the biryani. In a serving dish, place a layer of rice to start.
2. Add chicken pieces on top of the rice. Add a little bit of gravy from the chicken to flavor the rice.
3. Continue the process of laying a layer of rice following by a layer of chicken and gravy until you are almost done with both the rice and the chicken. Don't add too much gravy as the dish will become wet.
4. If you want the rice and chicken to mix more evenly, you can use a spoon to do so. However, in my experience, mixing them up while layering is enough and sometimes they get mixed as you are serving.

### Serving

1. The left over gravy can be used as a side dish and served alongside the main dish. 
2. Serve the biryani hot with the gravy and yogurt salad. 
3. You can also provide lime wedges and raw onion slices as garnish.
